Roles and permissions give you precise control over what each member of your AiSensa organization can access and perform. AiSensa ships with a set of built-in roles to cover common use cases, and also allows you to create fully custom roles tailored to your team's structure. As an administrator, you can assign and adjust roles at any time from the Roles & Permissions settings panel.

---
